The stories linked above depict relationships between demographic information and success outcomes for NYC high schools.

In the visualizations in this section, each dot represents one school. For each school, the demographic and success information has been averaged from a series of cohorts: the visualizations are derived from the merged dataset that was created after groupby was used in Pandas on the various demographic and success metric features. That is to say, whereas the original datasets contained information about many cohorts over the years from a given school, that information was averaged, by school, to create one row per school for visualization.

The visualizations show strong relationships between various economic metrics, like the Economic Need Index, and success metrics like a school's dropout rate. Please view the stories above for more information, and please utilize the filtering features on the right of each visualization for more information.

These visualizations were created in Tableau and are hosted in Tableau Public.
