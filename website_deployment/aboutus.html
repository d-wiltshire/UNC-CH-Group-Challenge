Our Team

Our team is comprised of four members (in alphabetical order): Swarup Dinesh, Caroline Hensley-Bonner, Divi Sharma, and David Wiltshire.

This project was undertaken as a team challenge as part of the University of North Carolina - Chapel Hill Certificate in Data Analytics program in August 2022. This was a 24-week intensive program focused on gaining technical programming skills in Excel, VBA, Python, R, JavaScript, SQL Databases, Tableau, Big Data, and Machine Learning.
